Branding

As a designer it is important to create a personal brand which is cohesive and recognisable, so I decided to redesign my portfolio website with this in mind. My portfolio website was developed using HTML, CSS and vanilla JavaScript, and uses a modern and simplistic colour scheme and typeface, which makes it clean, bold and easy to navigate. I also redesigned my CV, business cards and other aspects of my work to create a cohesive personal brand.
