Graphic design

Healthcare

This design project involved updating and redesigning handouts that were being used in an NHS mentalisation-based treatment group programme. The aim of the design was to be eye-catching and clear, and most importantly easily readable for people with difficulty reading and concentrating on large blocks of text. For this reason, the text was broken up into manageable sections and more images were used as an aid to help illustrate the concepts and break up large blocks of text. The blue colour scheme is both calming and in keeping with the NHS's brand image, and yellow details were added for contrast and to break up the otherwise monochromatic design.
