Trust in
News on
Social Media

dissertation study - web, 2022

Trust in News on Social Media was a study conducted as part of my University dissertation, looking into young people's attitudes towards trust in social media. The study was conducted via a website and interactive chatbot, developed primarily using JavaScript, through which a questionnaire was administered to participants. The website was made accessible for desktop and mobile. User interaction and accessibility were key focuses of the website, with a simplistic design and layout, minimalistic typeface and different readability options.
