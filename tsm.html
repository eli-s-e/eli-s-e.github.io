Research

Trust in Social Media was a study conducted as part of my University dissertation, looking into young people's attitudes towards trust in social media. In particular, I chose to study students' trust in news shared on social media. The study was conducted via a website and interactive chatbot used to deliver a survey to participants, which was developed using HTML, CSS and JavaScript, with MySQL database used to securely store participants' answers. The website was designed to be simple and accessible for students, and was made responsive for both desktop and mobile devices. Since user interaction and accessibility were key focuses of the website, a simplistic design and layout were used, and the console typeface gave the website the desired retro feel, as well as making text more readable for participants with reading imparements.
