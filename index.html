Hi, I'm Elise. 👋

I'm a freelance web and graphic designer based in Essex, UK. I have over 3 years of practical web design experience, and in 2022 I graduated with a BSc (Hons) in Computer Graphics, Imaging and Multimedia from the University of East Anglia.

I love to create beautiful, interactive websites, primarily working with HTML, CSS and modern JavaScript libraries. My background in studying UX enables me to focus on creating designs that are not only visually appealing, but also accessible and intuitive to use.

I also offer graphic design for branding such as social media banners, posters and business cards.
